About Us

Kirtase Hidayah is an Islamic platform dedicated to Dawah Ilallah — calling both Muslims and non-Muslims to the path of Allah through wisdom, compassion, and strategic outreach. We focus on educational programs, youth engagement, and online activities.

Our team consists of dedicated scholars, educators, and volunteers who are committed to spreading the message of Islam with clarity, wisdom, and compassion.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a guiding light for seekers of truth through knowledge and faith, illuminating hearts with the wisdom of Islam.

Our Mission

To build a community empowered with Islamic values, understanding, and unity — spreading the message of peace and purpose through education and action.
